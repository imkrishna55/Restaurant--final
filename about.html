About Us

Hot & Tasty is a leading restaurant in Nepal, offering a delightful dining experience with a wide variety of delicious dishes. Founded in 2000, our restaurant has been serving authentic Nepali and international cuisine to our valued customers for over two decades.

Our mission is to provide high-quality food using fresh ingredients sourced locally, combined with exceptional service in a warm and inviting atmosphere. We are committed to creating memorable experiences for our guests, ensuring they leave satisfied and eager to return.

Over the years, Hot & Tasty has become a favorite dining destination for both locals and tourists alike. With our talented chefs, attentive staff, and dedication to excellence, we strive to exceed our customers' expectations and set the standard for dining in Nepal.
